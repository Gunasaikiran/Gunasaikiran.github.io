About

I am a Modem Systems Engineer with a strong interest in wireless communications, 5G/6G systems. I completed my M.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering from the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore, where I worked on Reconfigurable Intelligent Surfaces (RIS) and real-time 5G NR systems. Prior to that, I earned my B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering from IIIT Nagpur.

My interests lie at the intersection of communication theory, system-level simulations, and real-world experimentation. Through my academic and industrial experiences, I have worked on wireless communication systems, FPGA-based designs, RIS-assisted networks, and modem technologies. I enjoy transforming research ideas into practical engineering solutions and continuously exploring emerging technologies.
